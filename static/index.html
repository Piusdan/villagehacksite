What is #VillageHack?

VillageHack is an initiative spearheaded by Beyonde Labs, that aims at taking the tech action down to the African Villages.

We are starting off with our inaugural event this December at Lugulu Village, in Western Kenya.

DATE: Dec 27, 2014, 8am - 6pm
VENUE: Lugulu Boarding Primary School View Map
Register — Registration opens on 3rd Dec.
